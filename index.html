St Keanu

The number of deaths from simple human indifference is growing. Have you ever wondered when you last helped the needy or someone else? There is a person whose actions will make you think about such concepts as generosity and care.

Keanu Reeves is one of the most controversial actors of modern Hollywood. However, Keanu isn’t just a talented actor, but also an amazingly sensitive, caring person and an outstanding personality. Keanu Reeves is one of those people who are in some way saints. Why a saint?

In everyday life, he helps the homeless, and sometimes spends whole days with them. Keanu has donated most of his savings to charity in various funds. Reeves has also started his own fund to fight cancer. He’s helped many of his colleagues if they were in trouble.

Despite the many misfortunes that have happened in his life, he gave a lot to other people, even if he wasn’t personally acquainted with this person. Keanu Reeves is a good example for all of us. I want more young people to be like him in the future.
